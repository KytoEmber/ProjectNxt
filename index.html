📝 Exam Rules & Info

🔒 Focus Mode

Tab switching is restricted. Leaving the tab may end your exam automatically. Integrity is key!

⏱ Timers

Questions have individual timers as reminders. If it runs out, you can still answer, but the total exam timer will force a submission when it hits zero.

🚫 One Choice Only

Once you pick an answer, it's final. You cannot change your selection anymore, so choose wisely!

🏆 Medals

Competitive (Hard) mode is required for Diamond medals. Platinum requires a 3-exam winning streak!
